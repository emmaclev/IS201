About Me

I grew up loving to play different sports such as soccer, basketball, cross-country, rugby, and volleybal. I enjoy hanging out with friends, spending time in the moutains, hiking, camping, fishing, reading, and singing.

I joined my siblings lawn mowing buisness, Cougar Lawn Care, when I was 14. I took over the buiness when I was 16. I continued the lawn mowing buisness while also working as a front desk receptionist at Boise Vision Care. I stoped working at those jobs when I moved away to University, where I started working at milk and cookies. These Jobs taught me how to work hard, be respectiful, self disciplined, and work with/communicate with people from all different backgrounds. I learned how to problem-solve and be efficent in a job.

I strive to bring postive and upbeat energy in the workplace! I believe all work is better with a good attitude and a smile on your face!
